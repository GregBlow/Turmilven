====== Grey Ooze ======
Created Monday 11 March 2019

**AC:** 13 (natural, shield)
**Hit points:** 18 (4d8)
**Speed:** 30ft/t, swim 30ft/t
**Str:** 13 (+1) **Dex:** 10 (0) **Con:** 11 (0) **Int:** 11 (+0) **Wis:** 11 (0) **Cha:** 8 (-1)
**Skills:** Perception +4
**Senses:** Darkvision 120ft , Passive Perception 14
**Languages:** Undercommon

Challenge 1/4 (50 XP)

**Amorphous: **The ooze can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

**Corrode Metal: **Any nonmagical weapon made of metal that hits the ooze corrodes. After dealing damage, the weapon takes a permanent and cumulative -1 penalty to damage rolls. If its penalty drops to -5, the weapon is destroyed. Nonmagical ammunition made of metal that hits the ooze is destroyed after dealing damage.

The ooze can eat through 2-inch thick, nonmagical metal in 1 round. 

**False Appearance:** While the ooze remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from an oily pool or wet rock.

**Actions:**

**Pseudopod: **MeleeWeapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5ft, one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) acid damage, and if the target is wearing nonmagical metal armor, its armor is partly corroded and takes a permanent and cumulative -1 penalty to the AC is offers. The armor is destroyed if the penalty reduces its AC to 10.

**Tactics:**

Skirmish preference, Disengage at 6hp, re-engage at full health. Shrieks if hit by cold/fire/necrotic damage, causing allies to swarm damage dealer. Whistles at <3hp, if half of group are whistling, rout.
