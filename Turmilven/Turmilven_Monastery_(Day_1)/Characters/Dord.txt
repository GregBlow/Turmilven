====== Dord ======
Created Tuesday 05 March 2019

Elderly Monk,well respected by all, severe. Blind

**Motivation:** Subjective idealism zealot. Believes any materialism is heretical or, at best, sinful. Controls activities of monastery by exerting influence on network of informally subordinate monks. Is not mad, has knowledge of the properties of ink but is unaffected (does not read). Manipulates abbot, despising them.
