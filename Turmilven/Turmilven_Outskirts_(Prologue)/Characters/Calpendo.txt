====== Calpendo ======
Created Thursday 07 March 2019

Level 8 Warlock pretending to be a wizard. Is client of a night hag. This deception is not to be revealed in this scenario, should use only spells available to both a wizard and a warlock with pact of tome. Is travelling to abbey for meeting in role of facilitator and mediator between local nobles and central government. Has pair of glasses (eyes of minute seeing) to be used when reading. Has Homonculus who acts as valet. Raven familiar.
