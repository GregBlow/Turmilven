====== Other Vegepygmies ======
Created Thursday 07 March 2019

All other vegepygmies should not be named, in off chance names are required use 2 syllable max Polynesian/Celtic/Norse/Mesoamerican names or Giles. Will defer to chief and druid in all cases except to flee. Convincing otherwise will lead to existential dread.
